\paragraph{3.4}~{}

由题意知$E[X_i] = \frac{X_{i-1}+1}{2}$
$$
\begin{aligned}
    E[Y_i|Y_0,Y_1,Y_2, \cdots ,Y_{i-1}] &= E[2^i(1-X_i)|Y_0,Y_1,Y_2, \cdots ,Y_{i-1}] \\
    &= 2^iE[1-X_i|Y_0,Y_1,Y_2, \cdots ,Y_{i-1}] \\ 
    &= 2^i(1-\frac{X_{i-1}+1}{2}) \\
    &= 2^{i-1}(1-X_{i-1}) \\
    &= Y_{i-1}
\end{aligned}
$$

所以$Y_0,Y_1 \cdots $是一个鞅。
